OUR TALE

The country is our home. The Hudson Foundry is a boutique agency that specializes in making good ideas happen. From the seed of a concept, we cultivate business plans, design experiences, then manufacture and operate them on a human scale.

Our current family of next-gen technology ventures span real estate, fashion, finance and media. Our projects are not just mere clients — we are building an ecosystem of mindful enterprises with scaling and collaborative futures in mind.

For us, the Hudson Valley is more than a destination for urban escape, it’s truly an innovative hub for creative and engineering minds who have chosen to live their professional lives in serenity among the trees.

PROJECTS

All of our work is home grown, free range, and stone ground. The best part? Being country-bound let's us deliver enterprise quality craftsmanship stress free, on-budget, and on-time.

COMPUSA

2020 re-imagination of a legacy brands digital offering

A forward-looking UX/UI redesign of desktop and mobile sites, redevelopment of entire stack, online app, mobile site, and new API automation for aggregating + filtering deals from multiple sources.

ZERO VACANCY

A pioneering advertising platform for real-estate discounts

We built a new platform with landlords across the country to offer deals on rent to the folks who help make our cities strong. The software included a consumer-friendly brand image and a complete geo-locating mobile site.

INFACE MEDIA

A proprietary digital out-of-home video and advertising network

Designed & launched a 200+ location digital signage combination network including custom designed and manufactured screens, and ongoing digital media management from creation to management.

WOLF + LAMB

Wolf + Lamb Hudson Valley Event Brands

We designed, branded and co-produced a successful, ongoing series of exciting country events. Each event was carefully calibrated in both color, design and tone for the various communities to which they were focused.

MILLIONAIRE

A luxury eco-concept brand that redefines “the rich life”

Developed entirely in-house, this trend transcendent, skillfully crafted, and ethically produced brand offered collections about everyday pleasure—quality that feels good in your hand and spirit.

TUBBYS

A local bar with a lot to say

We redesigned Tubby’s website with a custom site to showcase their incredible, varied menu, musical offerings and whim. The core of the site was built on customer-friendly Squarespace allowing self-upkeep.
